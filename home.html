Our Programmes

We focus on key areas to create a ripple effect of positive change that transforms lives.

Winger Global School

Providing education to disadvantaged communities, from basic literacy to vocational training.

Winger Global Hospital

Improving the health and well-being of underserved populations by providing access to basic medical services.

Women empowerment

Helping women become self-reliant with resources for health, legal rights, and entrepreneurship.

Winger Global Kitchen

Establishing community kitchens to provide nutritious meals and support the most vulnerable members of society.

Winger Global Water

Improving access to safe and clean water by building wells, treatment facilities, and other infrastructure.

Old Age

Providing social, emotional, and healthcare support to meet the needs and rights of the elderly.
