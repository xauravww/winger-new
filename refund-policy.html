Refund Policy

Last Updated: September 10, 2025

HopeWave is grateful for every donation we receive. Donations are the lifeblood of our organization, enabling us to carry out our vital work. This policy outlines the circumstances under which we may provide a refund for a donation.

1. General Policy

As a registered non-profit organization, we deploy resources as soon as they are received to support our ongoing projects. Therefore, all donations made to HopeWave are generally non-refundable. We ask that you make your decision to donate carefully.

2. Exceptions for Refund

We understand that errors can occur. We are happy to consider a refund for a donation under the following circumstances:

  • Error in Donation Amount: If you have made an error in the amount of your donation, please notify us within 7 days of the transaction.
  • Duplicate Donation: If you have accidentally made a duplicate donation, we will refund the duplicate amount. Please notify us within 7 days.
  • Unauthorized Donation: If you believe your card has been used for a donation without your authorization, please contact us immediately to report the unauthorized transaction.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please send an email to contact@hopewave.org with the subject line "Refund Request". Your request must include:

  • Your full name and contact information.
  • The date of the donation.
  • The transaction ID or reference number.
  • The reason for the refund request.

All refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Approved refunds will be processed within 15 business days and returned to the original method of payment.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at contact@hopewave.org.
